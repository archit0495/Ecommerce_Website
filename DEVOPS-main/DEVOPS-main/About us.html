About us


TILESBAZAAR has the unique distinction of being the first modern sale of Tiles platform established in the country.


The organisation owes its existence to the foresight and vision of its beloved founders.


Providing quality Premium Tiles and organising this sector in the country was their vision for the development and national prosperity.


The company maintains high quality standards, the certification by the International Organization for Standardization (ISO 9001) bears testimony for the same.



About Our Work


TILESBAZAAR brings to you premium Marble, Granite and Ceramic tiles that are detailed with perfection. Each tile is made using state of the art technology making them strong, durable & easy to maintain.
